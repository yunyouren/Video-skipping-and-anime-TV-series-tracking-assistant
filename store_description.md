## 详细说明 / Detailed Description

### 中文

#### 核心功能

**全能视频跳略与追番助手** 是一款专为追剧补番打造的效率工具，彻底告别重复拖拽进度条的痛苦。

##### 智能跳略
- **自动跳过片头片尾**：可自定义跳过秒数，到达时间点自动跳转
- **完播自动重置**：开启后视频播放完毕自动回到开头，无缝二刷
- **自动切下一集**：片尾处自动点击下一集按钮，连播不中断
- **短视频保护**：设置最短时长阈值，避免误跳过短视频

##### 追番收藏
- **一键收藏**：打开弹窗点击"+ 收藏当前"，自动识别番剧名、集数、当前进度
- **智能文件夹**：按"补番中""日漫""国漫"或星期几分组管理，支持新建/删除/移动
- **进度自动更新**：观看过程中自动同步进度，打开收藏即可续播
- **仅记录最大集数**：防止重温旧集时覆盖最新进度

##### 精准控制 — 白名单模式（v4.3 默认开启）
- **仅对已收藏生效**：未收藏的视频不会触发任何跳过行为，避免干扰直播、短视频等场景
- **每个收藏独立设置**：每个番剧可单独配置片头/片尾跳过秒数、短视频保护阈值、开关状态
- **即改即存**：所有设置自动保存，无需手动点击保存按钮

##### 效率工具
- **自定义快捷键**：支持录制快进/快退组合键（默认 Shift+→ / Shift+←）
- **站点标签规则**：自定义域名或标题关键词匹配，修正 Iframe 播放器的站点识别
- **番剧名称修正**：标题识别不准时，可设置规则强制指定番剧名
- **数据备份**：按文件夹选择性导出/导入 JSON，轻松迁移或分享收藏

##### 支持站点
哔哩哔哩（B站合集/列表深度优化）、爱奇艺、腾讯视频、优酷、芒果TV、YouTube、樱花动漫 等

##### 隐私声明
所有数据仅存储在浏览器本地，**绝不上传**任何服务器。权限仅用于视频控制和标题解析。

### English

#### Core Features

**Video Skip & Anime Tracker** is an efficiency tool built for binge-watchers. Stop dragging the progress bar — let the extension handle it.

##### Smart Skipping
- **Auto-skip intro/outro**: Set custom skip durations in seconds — automatically jumps at the right moment
- **Auto-restart on finish**: Video loops back to the beginning automatically when it ends
- **Auto-play next episode**: Clicks the "next episode" button for you at the outro
- **Short video protection**: Set a minimum duration threshold to prevent false triggers on clips

##### Favorites & Progress Tracking
- **One-click bookmark**: Captures series name, episode number, and current timestamp with a single click
- **Smart folders**: Organize by status ("Watching", "Completed") or by genre — create, rename, delete, move
- **Auto progress sync**: Watched progress updates in real time; click any favorite to resume where you left off
- **Max-episode guard**: When revisiting older episodes, won't overwrite your latest progress

##### Whitelist Mode (v4.3, enabled by default)
- **Favorites only**: Auto-skip only activates for series you've bookmarked — no interference with live streams, short videos, or other content
- **Per-series settings**: Each favorite remembers its own intro/outro skip times, duration threshold, and on/off state
- **Auto-save**: Every setting change is saved instantly — no save button needed

##### Power Tools
- **Custom keyboard shortcuts**: Record your own key combos for fast-forward and rewind (default: Shift+→ / Shift+←)
- **Site tag rules**: Fix site detection for iframe-based players via custom domain/title keyword matching
- **Series name rules**: Correct misidentified series titles with custom match-and-replace rules
- **Data import/export**: Selectively export favorites by folder as JSON — migrate or share your collection

##### Supported Sites
Bilibili (deep integration with collections/playlists), iQIYI, Tencent Video, Youku, MGTV, YouTube, Yinghua CD, and more

##### Privacy
All data is stored locally in your browser. **Nothing is ever uploaded** to any server. Permissions are strictly for video control and title parsing.
